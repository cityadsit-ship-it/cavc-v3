## 1Ô∏è‚É£ Managing Services

### What You Can Do:
- Add new advertising services
- Edit existing service descriptions
- Add or remove service images
- Upload PDF brochures for each service

### How to Add a New Service:

**Step 1: Go to Services Manager**
1. Click **"Services Manager"** in the left menu
2. You'll see all your current services listed

**Step 2: Click "Add New Service"**
1. Click the blue **"Add New Service"** button
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Title** - Name of the service (e.g., "LED Billboards")
   - **Description** - Brief description (1-2 paragraphs)
   - **Folder Name** - Simple name without spaces (e.g., "led-billboards")
3. Click **"Save"**

**Step 3: Add Images to the Service**
1. Find your new service in the list
2. Click **"Manage Gallery"** button
3. Click **"Add New Item"**
4. Fill in:
   - **Image Caption** - Short description
   - **Detailed Description** - Longer description for the popup
   - **Location** (optional)
   - **Dimensions** (optional)
   - **Features** (optional)
5. Click **"Choose File"** and select an image from your computer
6. Click **"Save"**

**Step 4: Upload a PDF Brochure (Optional)**
1. Go to **"PDF Manager"** in the left menu
2. Scroll to "Service PDFs"
3. Select your service from the dropdown
4. Click **"Choose File"** and select your PDF
5. Click **"Upload Service PDF"**

### How to Edit an Existing Service:

1. Go to **"Services Manager"**
2. Find the service you want to edit
3. Click the **"Edit"** button (pencil icon)
4. Make your changes
5. Click **"Save"**

### How to Delete a Service:

1. Go to **"Services Manager"**
2. Find the service you want to delete
3. Click the **"Delete"** button (trash icon)
4. Confirm deletion when asked

**‚ö†Ô∏è Warning:** Deleting a service removes all its images and data. This cannot be undone!

## 2Ô∏è‚É£ Managing Service Images (Gallery)

### How to Add Images to a Service:

**Step 1: Open Gallery Manager**
1. Go to **"Services Manager"**
2. Click **"Manage Gallery"** on the service you want to update

**Step 2: Add a New Image**
1. Click **"Add New Item"**
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Image Caption** - Main title (e.g., "EDSA Guadalupe Billboard")
   - **Detailed Description** - What people see when they click the image
   - **Location** - Where it's located (e.g., "EDSA Guadalupe")
   - **Dimensions** - Size (e.g., "15m x 8m")
   - **Features** - Special features (e.g., "LED, Double-sided")
3. Click **"Choose File"** and select your image
4. Click **"Save"**

**Tip:** Use high-quality images (at least 1920x1080 pixels) for best results.

### How to Edit an Image:

1. In the Gallery Manager, click **"Edit"** on the image
2. You can:
   - Change the caption or description
   - Replace the image (upload a new one)
   - Update location or dimensions
3. Click **"Save"**

### How to Delete an Image:

1. In the Gallery Manager, click **"Delete"** on the image
2. Confirm deletion when asked

### How to Reorder Images:

Images appear on your website in the order you added them. To change the order:
1. Delete and re-add images in your preferred order
2. OR (easier): Delete the last image, then the second-to-last, etc., and re-add them in the order you want

**Tip:** The first image in a service gallery appears as the main preview image on your website.

## 3Ô∏è‚É£ Managing Locations

### What You Can Do:
- Add new office locations
- Add new site locations (where your ads are)
- Update addresses and coordinates
- Delete old locations

### How to Add a New Office Location:

**Step 1: Go to Locations Manager**
1. Click **"Locations Manager"** in the left menu
2. You'll see two tabs: "Offices" and "Sites"

**Step 2: Add Office**
1. Make sure you're on the **"Offices"** tab
2. Click **"Add New Office"**
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Name** - Office name (e.g., "Main Office - Makati")
   - **Address** - Full street address
   - **Latitude** - For map (e.g., 14.5547)
   - **Longitude** - For map (e.g., 121.0244)
4. Click **"Save"**

**How to find Latitude and Longitude:**
1. Go to https://www.google.com/maps
2. Find your location on the map
3. Right-click on the exact spot
4. Click on the coordinates (they look like: 14.5547, 121.0244)
5. Copy and paste into the form

### How to Add a New Site Location:

Sites are where your advertisements are displayed (billboards, transit ads, etc.)

**Step 1: Go to Sites Tab**
1. In **"Locations Manager"**, click the **"Sites"** tab
2. Click **"Add New Site"**

**Step 2: Fill in Details**
1. **Name** - Site name (e.g., "EDSA Guadalupe Billboard")
2. **Address** - Where it's located
3. **Latitude** and **Longitude** - For map
4. Click **"Save"**

### How to Edit or Delete Locations:

1. Go to **"Locations Manager"**
2. Choose "Offices" or "Sites" tab
3. Click **"Edit"** to change details OR **"Delete"** to remove

## üì∏ Image Best Practices

### For Best Results:

**Image Size:**
- Minimum: 1920x1080 pixels (Full HD)
- Recommended: 2560x1440 pixels or higher
- File size: Under 5MB per image

**Image Format:**
- Use: JPEG (.jpg) for photos
- Use: PNG (.png) for graphics with text
- Use: WebP (.webp) for best web performance

**Image Quality:**
- Use bright, clear, high-resolution photos
- Avoid blurry or dark images
- Show your advertisements prominently
- Include context (location, surroundings)

**Photography Tips:**
1. Take photos during daylight for best colors
2. For LED/illuminated ads, also take nighttime photos
3. Show scale (include surroundings, people, or vehicles)
4. Use landscape orientation (horizontal) not portrait
5. Avoid logos or branding from competitors in photos

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Issues and Solutions

### "Login Failed" Error

**Problem:** Can't log into admin panel

**Solutions:**
1. Check your username and password (check for typos)
2. Make sure Caps Lock is OFF
3. Try resetting your password (contact your developer)
4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser

### Images Not Uploading

**Problem:** Image upload fails or shows error

**Solutions:**
1. Check file size - must be under 5MB
2. Check file type - must be JPG, PNG, or WebP
3. Try a smaller image
4. Try a different image format
5. Check your internet connection

### Changes Don't Appear on Website

**Problem:** You made changes but don't see them on the live site

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 1-2 minutes and refresh the page
2. Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
3. Try viewing in a private/incognito browser window
4. Check you're looking at the right page
5. Make sure you clicked "Save"

### PDF Not Downloading

**Problem:** Visitors can't download your PDF

**Solutions:**
1. Verify PDF was uploaded successfully (check PDF Manager)
2. Try re-uploading the PDF
3. Check PDF file isn't corrupted (open it on your computer first)
4. Make sure PDF is under 20MB

## üìù How to Make Simple Text Changes on GitHub

### Prerequisites:
- You must have access to your GitHub repository
- You must be logged into GitHub

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

**Step 1: Log into GitHub**
1. Go to https://github.com
2. Log in with your GitHub account
3. Go to your repository: `github.com/YOUR-USERNAME/cavc-website`

**Step 2: Find the File to Edit**

For different types of changes, edit these files:

| What to Change | File to Edit | Location |
|----------------|--------------|----------|
| Footer information | Footer.jsx | `src/components/Footer.jsx` |
| Contact information | MessageMe.jsx | `src/components/MessageMe.jsx` |
| About section | About.jsx | `src/components/About.jsx` |
| Hero headline | Hero.jsx | `src/components/Hero.jsx` |
| Location data | LocationData.js | `src/components/LocationData.js` |

**Step 3: Edit the File**
1. Click on the file name (e.g., `Footer.jsx`)
2. Click the **pencil icon** (‚úèÔ∏è) at the top right that says "Edit this file"
3. Make your changes to the text
4. **Important:** Only change text between quotes `"like this"`, don't change code

**Step 4: Save Your Changes**
1. Scroll to the bottom of the page
2. In the "Commit changes" box, write what you changed (e.g., "Updated phone number")
3. Click **"Commit changes"**

**Step 5: Wait for Update**
- Your website will automatically rebuild (takes 2-5 minutes)
- Check your live website to see the changes

## üìû How to Update Contact Information

### To Change Phone Number, Email, or Address:

**File to Edit:** `src/components/MessageMe.jsx`

**What to Look For:**

```javascript
const contactInfo = {
  phone: "+63 123 456 7890",      // ‚Üê Change phone here
  email: "info@cityadvph.com",     // ‚Üê Change email here
  address: "Your address here"     // ‚Üê Change address here
};
```

**How to Edit:**
1. Find the text between quotes `""`
2. Delete the old text
3. Type your new text
4. Keep the quotes `""`
5. Keep the comma `,` at the end of each line

**Example:**
```javascript
// Before:
phone: "+63 123 456 7890",

// After:
phone: "+63 917 555 1234",
```

## üìã Important Rules When Editing Files on GitHub

### ‚úÖ DO:
- Change text between quotes `"like this"`
- Keep all quotes, commas, and semicolons in place
- Write clear commit messages
- Check your website after changes

### ‚ùå DON'T:
- Delete any code or HTML tags `<div>`, `<p>`, etc.
- Remove quotes, commas, or semicolons
- Change variable names or function names
- Edit multiple files at once (do one at a time)
- Make changes without testing

## üìñ Glossary of Terms

**CMS** - Content Management System. The admin panel where you manage content.

**Dashboard** - The main screen you see when you log into the admin panel.

**Gallery** - Collection of images for a service.

**PDF** - Portable Document Format. Files that can be downloaded.

**Commit** - Saving changes on GitHub.

**Repository (Repo)** - Where your website code is stored on GitHub.

**Deploy** - Publishing your website changes to make them live.

**Cache** - Temporary storage in your browser. Sometimes needs to be cleared to see updates.
